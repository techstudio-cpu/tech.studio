About Green Harvest

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Green Harvest began as a small family farm stand with a simple mission: to provide our community with the freshest, most nutritious organic produce available.

Today, we partner with over 50 local organic farms to bring you farm-fresh fruits, vegetables, and products that are not only good for you, but also good for our planet.
