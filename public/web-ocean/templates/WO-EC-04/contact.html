Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver Monday through Saturday from 8AM to 8PM. Sunday delivery is available for orders over $75.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes! Orders placed before 2PM are eligible for same-day delivery within our delivery zone.

What's your return policy?

We accept returns within 24 hours of delivery for unopened, refrigerated items. Please contact us first.

Are all products organic?

Yes, all our products are certified organic. We also carry many non-GMO verified and fair trade items.
