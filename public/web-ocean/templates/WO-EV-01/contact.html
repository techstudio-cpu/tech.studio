FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we book?

For destination weddings we recommend 8–12 months of planning. For intimate celebrations, 4–6 months works beautifully.

Do you travel internationally?

Yes, our team has planned events in Italy, Thailand, Bali, and the Middle East. Travel logistics are handled end-to-end.

What budgets do you work with?

Our Essential package begins at ₹2.5L, with most multi-day celebrations ranging from ₹50L to ₹3Cr.

Can you work with our preferred vendors?

Absolutely. We love collaborating with new creatives while ensuring quality benchmarks are met.
