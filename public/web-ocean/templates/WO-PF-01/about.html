Hello, I'm Alex

Crafting Visual Stories That Connect

I'm a graphic designer based in San Francisco with over 8 years of experience helping brands tell their stories through thoughtful, strategic design. My work spans brand identity, web design, print, and illustration.

What drives me is the challenge of translating complex ideas into clear, compelling visuals. I believe great design isn't just about aesthetics‚Äîit's about solving problems, communicating effectively, and creating meaningful connections between brands and their audiences.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring SF's coffee scene, sketching in my Moleskine, or hiking the trails of Marin County. I'm always learning, always curious, and always excited about the next creative challenge.
