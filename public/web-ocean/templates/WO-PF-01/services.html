FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about working with me

What's your typical turnaround time?

Most projects take 2-4 weeks depending on scope and complexity. Rush projects can be accommodated with an additional fee. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

How many revisions are included?

All projects include 2-3 rounds of revisions. Additional revisions can be purchased if needed. My goal is to get it right, so I work closely with you throughout the process.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide via video calls, email, and project management tools. Distance has never been a barrier to creating great work.

What file formats will I receive?

You'll receive all source files (AI, PSD, etc.) plus export formats like PDF, PNG, JPG, and SVG. Everything you need for print and digital use.

What's your payment structure?

I require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments.

Do you offer ongoing design support?

Yes! I offer monthly retainer packages for clients who need ongoing design work. This is perfect for businesses with regular design needs.
