Our Story

Pawfect Supplies was born from a simple belief: every pet deserves to live a happy, healthy life. Founded in 2018 by Sarah and Michael Johnson, our journey began when their beloved golden retriever, Max, inspired them to create a one-stop destination for premium pet supplies.

What started as a small local store has grown into a community hub where pet lovers can find everything they need to care for their furry, feathered, and scaly companions. At Pawfect Supplies, we believe in giving back to the community that has supported us. Every year, we donate a portion of our profits to local animal shelters and rescue organizations. We've helped rehome over 2,000 pets and provided supplies worth more than $100,000 to animals in need.
