Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer grooming services?

Yes! We have professional groomers available by appointment. Our grooming services include bathing, nail trimming, ear cleaning, and styling.

Can I return products?

We offer a 30-day return policy on most products. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Some restrictions apply to perishable items.

Do you deliver?

Yes, we offer free delivery on orders over $50 within our local area. We also ship nationwide with various shipping options available.

How do I start the adoption process?

You can start by visiting our adoptions page or contacting us directly. We'll guide you through the application process and help you find your perfect match.

Do you accept walk-ins for adoptions?

Yes, we welcome walk-ins during store hours. However, we recommend calling ahead to ensure the pets you're interested in are available to meet.

Do you offer pet training classes?

We host regular training workshops and can recommend certified trainers in your area. Contact us for current class schedules and registration.
