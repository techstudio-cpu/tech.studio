# WO-EV-04: Luxury Party & Social Planners

## Template Name
Opaline Soirée — Luxury Party & Social Events

## Category
Events & Weddings > Party Planning

## Pages Included
1. **index.html** – Immersive hero, signature themes, curated experiences, testimonials.
2. **themes.html** – Theme gallery with immersive descriptions and décor specs.
3. **gallery.html** – Editorial-style photo sets with hover stories and vendor tags.
4. **checklist.html** – Interactive planning checklist and celebration timeline.
5. **vendors.html** – Preferred artisans, entertainment roster, and onboarding steps.
6. **book.html** – Inquiry form, availability calendar, and concierge packages.

## Color Palette
- Primary: #2E1D47 (Mulberry Night)
- Secondary: #E4B7FF (Crystal Lilac)
- Accent: #FFD580 (Champagne Glow)
- Background: #121018 gradient to #261A35

## Typography
- Headings: "Playfair Display", serif
- Body: "Manrope", sans-serif

## Features & Assets
- Glassmorphic cards, couture-inspired gradients, editorial hover overlays.
- Vendor highlight badges and timeline components reused across pages.
- Shared asset folder inherits `core/css` + `main.js` behaviors for back-to-top and nav toggle.
