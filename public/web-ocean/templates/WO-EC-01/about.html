Our Journey

From Passion to Fashion

Velvet & Thread was born from a simple belief: every woman deserves to feel confident and beautiful in what she wears. What started as a small boutique in Mumbai has grown into a beloved destination for fashion-forward women across India.

Our founder, Ananya Mehta, started this journey with a vision to bring together timeless elegance and contemporary style. With over 15 years in the fashion industry, she curates each piece with an eye for quality, craftsmanship, and wearability.

Today, we're proud to serve over 50,000 happy customers who trust us to help them look and feel their best for every occasion ‚Äì from everyday moments to life's most special celebrations.

Our Commitment

Fashion with a Conscience

We believe beautiful fashion shouldn't come at the cost of our planet. That's why we're committed to sustainable practices at every step of our journey.

  • Ethical Sourcing We partner with manufacturers who share our values of fair wages and safe working conditions.
  • Eco-Friendly Packaging All our packaging is recyclable or biodegradable, reducing waste one order at a time.
  • Quality Over Quantity We create pieces meant to last, reducing the cycle of fast fashion consumption.
  • Carbon Neutral Shipping We offset our shipping emissions through verified environmental projects.
