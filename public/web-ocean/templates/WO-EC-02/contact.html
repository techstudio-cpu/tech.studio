Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become an artisan partner?

Email us at artisans@artisanhaven.com with photos of your work, your story, and pricing information. We'll review your application within 5 business days.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship worldwide. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on weight, dimensions, and destination. International orders may be subject to customs fees.

How long do custom orders take?

Custom order timelines vary by complexity and artisan availability, typically ranging from 2-8 weeks. We'll provide a specific timeline during your consultation.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. Custom orders are final unless there's a defect in craftsmanship. Please contact us before returning any item.

Do you verify artisan quality?

Absolutely! Every artisan undergoes a thorough vetting process including portfolio review, quality standards assessment, and ethical business practices verification.

Can I visit your studio?

Yes! Our studio is open to visitors during business hours. We love showing people our space and introducing them to local artisans. Appointments are recommended.
