Meet the Chef

Chef Vikram Malhotra

Executive Chef & Culinary Director

With over 20 years of culinary experience spanning Michelin-starred kitchens in London and traditional Indian restaurants across the subcontinent, Chef Vikram brings a unique perspective to Indian cuisine.

Trained under legendary chefs in Delhi and Mumbai, he's mastered the art of balancing bold spices with subtle flavors. His philosophy: "Respect the ingredient, honor the tradition, but never fear innovation."
