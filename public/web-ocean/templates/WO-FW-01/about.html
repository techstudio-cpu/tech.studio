Our Beginning

Where Peace Meets Practice

Serenity Yoga was born from a simple vision: to create a welcoming space where everyone, regardless of experience or ability, could discover the transformative power of yoga.

Founded by Sarah Chen in 2015, our studio began in a small converted warehouse with just three classes a week. Sarah's dream was to build more than just a yoga studioâ€”she wanted to create a community where people could find balance, strength, and inner peace.

Today, we're proud to serve over 500 students weekly, offering diverse classes taught by experienced, certified instructors. While we've grown, our core mission remains unchanged: to make yoga accessible, authentic, and transformative for all.
