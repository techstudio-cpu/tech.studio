Questions

Membership FAQ

Everything you need to know

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes! Unlimited and Premium members can freeze their membership for up to 2 months per year for a small administrative fee of $15/month.

What's your cancellation policy?

Monthly memberships require 30 days notice to cancel. Annual memberships are non-refundable but can be frozen if needed.

Do class packs expire?

Class packs are valid for 3 months from purchase date. Extensions may be granted for medical reasons with documentation.

Can I share my membership?

Memberships are non-transferable. However, class packs can be shared with family members, and unlimited members receive guest passes.

What if I need to miss a class?

Please cancel at least 2 hours before class start time. Late cancellations or no-shows for unlimited members may result in a $10 fee.

Do you offer corporate memberships?

Yes! We offer special corporate rates for companies with 5+ employees. Contact us for custom pricing and wellness packages.
