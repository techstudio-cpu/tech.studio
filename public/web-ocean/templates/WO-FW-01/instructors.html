Lead Instructor
Founder & Director

Sarah Chen

E-RYT 500, Yoga Alliance Certified

Sarah founded Serenity Yoga in 2015 with a vision to create a welcoming space where everyone could experience the transformative power of yoga. With over 15 years of teaching experience and training in multiple yoga traditions, she brings depth, wisdom, and compassion to every class.

Her teaching style blends traditional Hatha principles with modern alignment techniques, making yoga accessible while honoring its ancient roots. Sarah has studied extensively in India and completed advanced trainings in anatomy, meditation, and yoga therapy.

Specialties:

Power Yoga Yoga Therapy Meditation Teacher Training

Certifications:

  • E-RYT 500 (Experienced Registered Yoga Teacher)
  • Certified Yoga Therapist (C-IAYT)
  • Advanced Anatomy & Physiology
  • Mindfulness Meditation Teacher

"Yoga is not about touching your toes. It's about what you learn on the way down."
